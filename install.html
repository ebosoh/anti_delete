Get Started in 7 Easy Steps

Follow this premium guide to install, configure, and activate antiDELETE on your Android phone.

01

Disable Play Protect

Open Play Store ⇒ Click profile ⇒ Play Protect ⇒ Scan Apps with Play Protect ⇒ Turn Off

02

Visit Website

Visit www.antidelete.com on your phone's web browser

03

Download APK

Click “Download Tiny APK 16KB” and then open the file to start installation

04

Allow Restricted Settings

Go to your Phone Settings ⇒ Apps ⇒ antiDELETE ⇒ Click the three vertical dots (⋮) ⇒ Select Allow “Access to Restricted Settings”

05

Enable Notification Access

Open the antiDELETE app on your phone ⇒ tap Allow "Access to Notifications"

06

Get Activation Code

Copy your unique Device ID inside the app & share it on WhatsApp (+254780010010) to receive your personal Activation Code
