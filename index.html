⚠️ Sideload & Installation Guide

Follow these steps if you experience warnings or restricted permissions during setup:

Play Protect: App Blocked Warning
  1. Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. At the top right, tap the profile icon.
  3. Tap Play Protect → Settings.
  4. Turn Scan apps with Play Protect off.
"Restricted Setting" Error (Android 13+)?
  1. Go to your phone's Settings → Apps.
  2. Search for and select antiDELETE.
  3. Tap the three vertical dots (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  4. Tap "Allow restricted settings".
  5. Authenticate using PIN/pattern or fingerprint.
  6. Now return to Notification Access and turn antiDELETE ON!

💡 Play Protect Dialog? Click "More details" → "Install anyway". antiDELETE is open-source, runs 100% locally on your phone, and has zero server uploads.
